  (devices `de3628b` reverts `b3c88ec`). 3-cycle hardware test
  showed the barrier destabilized boot — cycle 1 produced a 13 MB

  without `fbcon: ok`. Theory: the explicit dsb sy at the very
  top of xhci_capProbe interacts badly with the bridge's
